Earth: Fight Against Extinction

Earth: Fight Against Extinction is a 2D turn-based strategy rogue-lite. Players need to use their units to defend population centers against deformed, mechanized animals. Each turn they enemy units broadcast their actions before doing them. The player must react and use anything available to turn the tide of battle in their favor including terrain, movement, and damage attacks. Prevent the enemies from completing their intended actions or use their own actions against them. In Earth: Fight Against Extinction you're only limited by how creative you can be.

Earth: Fight Against Extinction is intended for release in mid 2019. Be sure to check back here regularly for developement updates including screenshots and more!
